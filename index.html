Frog Life Cycle

Hover over the images to learn more about the life stages of a frog

Egg

The first stage of a frog's life cycle is an egg. Frogs lay eggs in big floating clusters. After 1-3 weeks, the eggs will hatch into tadpoles.

Tadpole

Tadpoles have long tails for swimming underwater. After several weeks, the tadpole develops lungs and legs, becoming a froglet.

Froglet

The froglet's tail is significanlty shorter than when it was a tadpole and it now spends time outside of water as well. Soon, it will be an adult frog.

Adult Frog
